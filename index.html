My Approach

I know seeking therapy can be difficult and it can be hard to venture into the unknown. I believe it takes an incredible amount of courage to begin this journey of healing. I’m honored to be a part of this process. Optional Graphic,Display Services,List Professional Certifications

“Your visions will become clear only when you can look into your own heart. Who looks outside, dreams; who looks inside, awakes.”

— Carl Jung

I use a relational approach to therapy and believe that having a safe, nonjudgmental space to explore your hurt can be transformative. Through the therapeutic relationship we will work together to address your pain and begin healing. Often I have seen individuals seek therapy when they feel hopeless and cannot see through the darkness that things can be different. Through therapy I aim to reveal hope again and walk alongside you as we find the light together.

I am certified in the Neurosequential Model of Therapeutics, or NMT, developed by Dr. Bruce Perry. This approach to therapy looks at how your relationships and experiences have shaped who you are and how you see the world. Through NMT I have learned that positive, healthy relationships can be the greatest agents of change. I also have training in Trauma Focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (TF-CBT) and utilize interventions involving mindfulness and body awareness.
